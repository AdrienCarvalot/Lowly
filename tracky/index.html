Common Questions

Is Tracky free to use?

Tracky is free to download and includes a free tier for essential budgeting. You can unlock additional features with an optional one-time Pro upgrade or a subscription.

Does Tracky connect to bank accounts?

No. Tracky is a manual budget planner — we don't require or support bank connections.

How does iCloud sync work?

Tracky uses Apple's iCloud to sync your data across your iPhone and iPad. Your budgets never pass through our servers.

Does Tracky work offline?

Yes. Tracky works fully on-device, so you can use it offline. An internet connection is only needed if you want to sync your data via iCloud across devices.

Does Tracky support widgets?

Yes. Tracky includes Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets so you can check your budget at a glance.

Is my financial data private?

Yes. Tracky does not use third-party analytics or trackers, and your data stays on your device and your iCloud account.
